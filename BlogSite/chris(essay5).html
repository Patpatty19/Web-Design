The Possible Cause of a Slow Computer and its Effects

Christian I. Denzon
April 26, 2021

In this time of pandemic, we’ve leaned onto technology more, specifically computers. Using a computer is now part of our everyday life since people attend to their jobs there, students use it so that they can study online through the internet, and it is usually the place where most people get their news from because the internet is accessible anytime which means the news is up to date. However, some people may face a problem with their computers and that problem is the device being slow. By slow, I mean that the computer is lagging whenever something opens, and that they cannot create files because they do not have enough storage. In this essay, I will list down the possible causes of a slow computer and its effects. I strongly recommend to research about computers so that you don’t get scammed, since most of the time, when people experience their computers getting slow, they instantly replace the whole unit which is much more expensive than identifying the problem and if possible, upgrade that component instead of replacing the whole computer.

The first possible cause is quite common, and that is when the specifications of the computer itself is not really capable of handling the software that you use. The effect of a computer with incapable specifications is that it will lag whenever you try to open any software such as: browsers, Microsoft Word, MS Teams, Zoom, and many other software. Another effect that is in tandem with a bad computer is the computer operating really slow when you do tasks. As an example, in my experience, whenever I try to open software that is demanding for processing power, like a video editor, my computer chugs meaning that it slows down. This chug can be easily felt when you play graphic demanding games too.

The second possible cause is that you don’t have enough disk drive space or storage for files. The effect of not having enough storage space is the computer slows down when you don’t have enough free space because the computer creates files, delete them, and move files so that means that the computer needs enough free space to do that. Usually, the issue of not having enough space can be simple solved by emptying the recycling bin, and deleting temporary files.

The third possible cause is that you might have a malware or virus in your computer. Most viruses run in the background of the computer, meaning that you can’t actively see it and it also uses most of the performance of the computer. Because of that, the computer will slow down and run very sluggish. Some viruses can be easily removed from your computer by using antiviruses like Windows Defender and Malwarebytes.

There are many other possible causes for a slow computer but the three causes that I have discussed in this essay is part of the most common and reasonable cause out there. Learning the cause of slow computers and its effects is beneficial to us because we can come to a solution when we’re facing such problems. Now that we have the knowledge, we can decide whether to upgrade or replace the unit itself. In conclusion, we should take the time to be knowledgeable and responsible on our computer so that we can take care of it.
