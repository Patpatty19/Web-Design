Becoming a CIITeen: What Track and/or Strand to Pursue?

Patrick Miguel M. Babala
April 5, 2021

As we grow older and become more mature, we do not suffer from any childlike problems. Some of them can be still a problem but only a limited amount of it. One of the most important decision in our lives is what career should we pursue? As a student, tracks are the most important paths toward careers. Studying a certain track can help us students narrow down our choices of careers. If you have passion towards photography and multimedia, Media and Visual Arts track will do the thing for you. If 3d modeling and Storyboard are the works you want to do, Animation track will be best for you. If coding and software developing are your passion, then the Programming track is the one that is best for you. Each tracks are very different yet similar with each other. They can help you pursue a creative or artistic careers.

Media and Visual Arts track focuses on photography and multimedia art. These two things are different but connected with each other. Photography needs the help of multimedia, while multimedia needs the help of photography at some point. Specialization subjects of this track are digital photography, fundamentals of film and video production, writing for new media, and multimedia publishing. Once you have learned these particular subjects, some careers connected to this track are waiting. Career options are being a videographer, photographer, production designer, graphic designer illustrator, and a digital artist. Some of these careers are very in demand right now. Which means companies are searching for people that exceeds in this track. Logos, designs, billboards, and other designing work are an example of what job you could have if you pursue this track.

Graphic designs are one of the most important skill you need in the art industry. Animation track also teaches graphic design. While media and visual arts focuses on photography and multimedia art, animation track focuses on animation of 3d modeling made my animators. Animation is not the only thing they teach in this track, storyboard and layout are also being taught here. 3d modeling are nothing without its storyboard and layout. 2d and 3d perspective are what expert animators excel of. If you have the skill and talent to become an expert animator in the future then the careers you can have are: dynamic or animated web designer, storyboard artist, 2d and 3d layout artist, 3d animator, and 3d architectural designer. Companies that works on animation have a high amount of pay. Being an excellent animator takes so much skill which means being one of them is a must have for higher companies. Disney animators are an example of excellent animators.

Besides art related careers, a certain track is also being taught in CIIT. This is the Computer Programming track. As a student who had no programming background before in high school, this is the correct school to enroll to if you want to learn on how to code and program a software. Programmers at this day and age are very in demand. Only few have the skill to program and code. Option for a career in programming are: system programmer, system analyst, software application developer/engineer, graphics programmer, game designer, and senior interactive software developer. A programmer takes too much skill because you are dealing with the software of the computer. Some would argue as a programmer you are basically talking to the computer. Being a programmer is very hard and programming or coding is a very hard thing to do. Once you mastered the art of coding, creating a software is basically an easy task.

Each of these tracks are very different yet very similar with each other. They can all work in the same company. A video game company has all of this people that exceeds in multimedia, animation, and programming. Multimedia experts deal with character designs, Animation experts deal with the movement and 3d modeling of the environment of the game. They could also do the storyboard and layout. Programming experts combine both the characters and the movements in a particular software that runs the game. Each of these tracks work very well with each other. All of the completed works combined to a single completed work can represent a successful company. It could a video game company, advertising company, movie company and many more. Designing is one of the similar traits each of these tracks have. Each track has different specialization subjects and career options but are still connected with each other in a way. Without the help of the other track, work might pile up and has the chance of not finishing the task.
